Our Solar System

Our Sun was formed about 4.5 billion years ago!!!It was formed by when a cloud of interstellar gas and dust collapsed and formed our Solar System.All the eight planets were formed The planets are divided into two categories.They are Terrestrial & Jovian.

Terrestrial planets

Terrestrial planets include Mercury,Venus,Earth and Mars.They are made of rocky materials,they have solid surfaces and have few moons or no moons.The second categorie (Jovian) includes Jupiter,Saturn,Uranes and Neptune.They are divided in two forms. Gas giants and ice giants.They have multiple moons,they have rings and have no solid surfaces.
